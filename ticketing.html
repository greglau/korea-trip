Inter-City Transport (Book 1–2 Months Ahead)

SegmentDurationCost (KRW)
Incheon Airport → Seoul Station ~43 min ₩9,500 (AREX Regular)
Seoul → Singyeongju (KTX) ~2h15m ₩60,000–75,000
Singyeongju → Busan (KTX) ~30 min ₩18,000–25,000
Busan → Suseo (SRT) ~2h30m ₩65,000–80,000
Seoul Station → Incheon Airport ~43 min ₩9,500 (AREX Regular)
Korail (KTX): Book at letskorail.com — English site available. July is peak season; book as soon as tickets open (usually 30 days in advance).
SRT: Book at srail.co.kr — English site available. SRT from Busan to Suseo drops you in Gangnam (10-min taxi to COEX), saving 45–60 min vs KTX to Seoul Station + subway.
AREX: Can be bought at airport ticket machines or with T-money card. Reserved seat (₩2,000 extra) recommended for Day 1 arrival.

Critical Advance Bookings

Changdeokgung Secret Garden (Huwon) Tour
Guided tour of the royal retreat. Strict daily cap of 100 visitors per time slot. English tours available but limited — book the earliest slot for best light.
BOOK 6 DAYS AHEAD cdg.go.kr/english ₩3,000 entry + ₩5,000 tour 2.5 hours
Haeundae Sky Capsule
Miniature capsule train along Haeundae coast. Extremely popular — books out weeks in advance. Must reserve online.
BOOK 2–4 WEEKS AHEAD haeundaesky.com ₩16,500/person
ICML Conference Registration & Badge
Ensure your conference registration is complete and badge/passes are ready. COEX venue — walk from hotel.
CONFIRM NOW ICML 2025 website
Seoul → Incheon Airport (AREX Reserved Seat)
Reserved seat on AREX for departure day. ₩2,000 extra but guarantees space with luggage at ~12:30 PM.
BOOK 1 WEEK AHEAD ₩11,500 total

Optional but Recommended

T-money Card
Reloadable transit card for Seoul/Busan/Gyeongju subways, buses, and taxis. Buy at any convenience store (CU, GS25, 7-Eleven) or airport arrival hall. Load ₩30,000–50,000.
₩2,500 deposit + ₩30,000+ load Airport / convenience stores
Naver Maps / KakaoMap Download
Download offline maps for Seoul and Busan. Google Maps does NOT work well for Korean bus routing. Naver Maps has English UI option.
Free apps
Airport Lounge Access (Incheon)
3.5 hours before departure gives plenty of time, but Incheon lounges (Korean Air, Asiana, Plaza) are excellent. Consider purchasing day pass (~₩40,000–60,000) if you want a premium send-off.
