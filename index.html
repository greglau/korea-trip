Quick Tips

Download before you go:

  • Naver Maps — Google Maps doesn't work well for Korean bus routing. Naver has English UI.
  • T-money card — Buy at airport arrival hall. ₩2,500 deposit + ₩30,000 load covers all transit.
  • KakaoMap — Excellent backup navigation app.

Key transport:

  • KTX (Korail): Seoul→Gyeongju, Gyeongju→Busan — book at letskorail.com
  • SRT: Busan→Suseo (Gangnam) — book at srail.co.kr. Drops you 10 min from COEX.
  • AREX: Airport↔Seoul Station — 43 min, ₩9,500. Buy at airport machines.
